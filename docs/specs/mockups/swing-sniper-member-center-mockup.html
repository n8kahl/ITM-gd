Swing Sniper - Options Research & Adaptive Intelligence

Not a scanner. A member-center decision engine for options ideas.

This mockup locks the information hierarchy for Swing Sniper as a new members tab. The launch shape is a three-part workspace: a ranked opportunity board, a symbol dossier that explains why volatility may be mispriced, and a right rail with the Swing Sniper memo, saved theses, active monitoring, and scope guardrails. It is designed to stay clean and easy to navigate while still covering the full Swing Sniper system.
